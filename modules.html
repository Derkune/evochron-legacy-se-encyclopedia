Modules Items
The Shield Module provides additional recharge capability for your ship's shield system. It will recharge shield arrays at a rate that is about 10% faster than the base rate of the ship and energy bias setting. Assembly: 20
The Thruster Module adds power to your ship's maneuvering thruster system, resulting in a 15% increase over the base agility level of the ship its installed on. Assembly: 20
The Energy Module provides additional recharge capability for your ship's energy system. It will recharge your ship's energy system at about a 10% faster rate. Assembly: 20
The ECM Module enhances your ship's countermeasure system by increasing the level of output energy applied to each CM launched. It increases countermeasure effectiveness by about 25%. Assembly: 20
The Heatsink Module provides additional heat dissipation capability and increases the cooling rate by about 20%. Assembly: 20
